Multimedia

  • Multimedia is just mixing things like text, pictures, audio, video, and animations in one place.
  • It helps explain ideas better than plain text because people learn through different senses.
  • A page with visuals or a short clip is usually easier to understand than just paragraphs.
  • But too much media can slow down a site, so you have to pick what’s actually helpful.
  • Adding alt text, captions, and clear visuals keeps your site accessible for everyone.
  • When used well, multimedia makes learning smoother and websites more interesting.
