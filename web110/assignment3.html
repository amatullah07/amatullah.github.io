Types of SEOs

  1. White Hat SEOs work toward making your search relevant and follows the search engine optimization guidelines
  2. Black Hat SEOs use malicious ways and do no follow search engine optimization guidelines, mostly involving deception
  3. Gray Hat SEOs are the in between.
