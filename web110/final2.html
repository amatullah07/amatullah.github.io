Graphics and Multimedia

  • Computer graphics are just computer-made pictures, and they mainly come in two types.
  • Raster graphics are made of tiny colored squares. When you zoom in, you can see the little pixels.
  • Vector graphics are made from shapes and math, so they stay smooth no matter how big you make them.
  • Photos are usually raster because they need a lot of detail.
  • Logos and icons are often vector because they need to stay sharp on any screen.
  • Think of raster like phone photos and vector like clean cartoon-style logos.
